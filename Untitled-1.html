About the Conference

The 39th AROI Tamil Nadu and Pondicherry Chapter Conference will be held on September 20-21, 2024, at Christian Medical College, Vellore. The theme is "Transforming Radiation Oncology: Embracing Compassion, Technology, and Quality Care."

Pre-Conference Workshops

  • Gynecology Brachytherapy: September 19, 1 PM - 4 PM
  • Plan Evaluation in Radiotherapy: September 20, 8 AM - 12 PM
